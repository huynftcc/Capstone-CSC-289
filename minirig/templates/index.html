Understanding ITX Builds

Form Factor Comparison

ITX motherboards measure just 6.7" × 6.7" (170mm × 170mm), compared to ATX at 12" × 9.6" (305mm × 244mm) and mATX at 9.6" × 9.6" (244mm × 244mm).

These compact dimensions enable significantly smaller cases while still supporting powerful CPUs and GPUs.

Common Challenges

Heat management requires careful planning in compact cases. Component compatibility can be more restrictive due to space constraints.

Our compatibility checker helps you navigate these challenges with confidence.
